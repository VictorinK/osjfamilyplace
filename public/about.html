About Us

About OSJ Family Place

With many years of providing innovative solutions that improve health and quality of life for those in need of in-home health services, OS Family Place has established itself as one of the most experienced homecare companies in the industry. Our approach to quality care focuses on a commitment to providing a level of expertise, training, patient service, and monitoring that is difficult to match in our industry. Featured by Assisted Living Magazine as one of the best communities in Orlando. We are licensed in the state of Florida. Our license # 171242216.

Benefits of working with us

Experience the fulfilling world of nursing, where compassion, skill development, and meaningful connections pave the way for a brighter future.

  • Certified home healthcare programs.
  • Pre-employment screening process to help us select qualified professionals.
  • Experienced and credentialed caregivers.
  • Our staff is on call 24 hours a day, 7 days a week to provide the most timely and efficient services possible.
